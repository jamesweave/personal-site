About Me

Hey! I'm James; I'm a Graphic Communications student at Clemson University, and I love design! I specialize in vector graphics and cartoon illustrations, but I enjoy any work I can get my hands on. Web design/ development is my field of choice, as it combines my left-brained talent for problem solving and designing for a purpose with my right-brained aptitude for doing so creatively and making it look pretty.

A little about my personal life: I grew up in Easley, SC and moved a whole twenty minutes away to attend Clemson. I love music, whether it be playing the guitar or banjo or listening to anything from Grateful Dead to Anderson Paak (check out some of my work for my student-run record label). I keep busy by running, fishing, and enjoying nice beer to offset my otherwise healthy lifestyle. I love movies, sports, and video games, so let's talk about those!
