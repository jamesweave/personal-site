Drinking Buddy

UX Design • Visual Design • Prototyping

Drinking Buddy is a conceptual app that helps users to find drink deals at local bars. For this case study, I developed user personas to come up with the best functionality for who's using it and how. After doing research and wireframing, I created a high fidelity design comp and protoype using Adobe XD.
